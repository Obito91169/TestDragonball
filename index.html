Goku

Goku is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball metaseries.

He is a Saiyan, originally named Kakarot, who was sent to Earth as a baby and raised by his adoptive grandfather, Son Gohan.

Vegeta

As the prince of a demolished Planet Vegeta, Vegeta is initially introduced as an unrepentant villain. Judging from his evil intentions before joining the Z Fighters, Vegeta has been consistently characterized as extremely arrogant, vengeful, and at times, sadistic.

Piccolo

Piccolo from Dragon Ball is a Namekian, a green-skinned, humanoid alien with a strong connection to the Dragon Balls and a history of both rivalry and alliance with Goku. Initially introduced as a villain seeking revenge, Piccolo becomes a key ally, mentor to Gohan, and a powerful fighter in the Z Fighters' battles against various threats.

Freeza

Frieza is a major antagonist in the Dragon Ball franchise, known for his immense power, cruelty, and role as a galactic tyrant. He's the main antagonist of the Frieza Saga and a recurring villain throughout the series, often seeking more power and revenge. Frieza is feared for his ruthlessness and is one of the most powerful beings in Universe 7.

Broly

Broly is a powerful Saiyan with a unique and uncontrollable power. He is a kind-hearted Saiyan, but his immense power can cause him to lose control and rampage when pushed too far.

Gohan

Gohan is known for his immense hidden potential as a Saiyan-human hybrid. He is the eldest son of Goku and Chi-Chi, and the older brother of Goten. He possesses incredible strength, speed, and durability, with his power often revealed during intense moments of rage or distress.

Gohan

Goten is a Saiyan and Earthling hybrid from the Dragon Ball universe, Goku and Chi-Chi's younger son, and Gohan's younger brother. He's known for his youthful appearance, kind heart, and his ability to transform into a Super Saiyan at a very young age. He's also adept at the Fusion Dance, allowing him to combine with Trunks to form Gotenks.
